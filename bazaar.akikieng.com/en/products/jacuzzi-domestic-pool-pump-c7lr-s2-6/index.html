Jacuzzi Domestic Pool Pump (C7LR-S2)

Description

- Patented air-cooled heat sink allows pump to run dry without damage to shaft seal. UNCONDITIONAL ONE-YEAR WARRANTY AGAINST SEAL FAILURE.

- Exclusive Ring-Lok Strainer allows "tool free" access to the strainer basket.

- Models with 12 o'clock position discharge are self-priming, models with 9 o'clock position discharge are non self-priming.

- Suction/discharge on 12 o'clock position models and suction on 9 o'clock position models include both 1 1/2" (38mm) FNPT and 1 1/2" (38mm) straight union connections (unions ordered separately).

- Discharge on 9 o'clock position models feature a 1 1/2" (38mm) swivel union connection exclusively for connection with Jacuzzi element and DE filter systems (unions ordered separately).

- Internal ribs in large strainer basket assure flow, even when filled with leaves and debris.

- 6 ft. (1,8 m) cord pumps feature a 3-prong grounded plug.

- 3 ft. (0,9 m) cord pumps feature an NEC twist lock plug (UL listed).

- Easy-access drain plug on front of strainer body for easy winterizing.

- Dual-voltage motors are standard on LR-S models making them ideal for in-ground pools.

- Two-speed motors offer optimum performance on high speed, and energy efficiency on low speed for normal heating and filtration.

- Maximum water temperature 104 F (40 C)
